What Is A Treatment?

They are two terms when referred to finding a solution or resolution to a problem in the medical field. A treatment is one of them, a resolution to an illness or disease, helps in controlling the illness until there is a cure available. Unlike a cure, the treatments do not make the patient's problems disappear. A treatment focuses more on controlling the illness as well as pain, the person will still have the illness throughout the treatments. Some treatments if followed thoroughly can lead to having the illness removed completely but it is not very likely. Not all treatments are the same; there are a variety of treatments that a person can go through depending on what illness or struggle they are dealing with. Most people believe treatments will take away their illness but in reality a treatment is only a temporary solution. It usually is just used to keep the patient's illness stable from becoming too much for that person to handle.



Benefits

  • Treatments generally would be that they are much easier to find for illness or diseases compared to cures.


  • Many different types of treatments that a patient can look into and check which fits best for them.



Disadvantages

  • Treatment it usually just puts a pause on the patient's illness and it would not get rid of it entirely

  • There might not be a Treatment for every disease

  • The process to look for treatments might be long depending on the illness
