About Me

My name is Leslie Calle and I am a software engineer who is intrested in the medical field. I am currently a sophmore in the Highschool Of Telecommunications Arts and Technology. I created this website to inform my audience on the differences of medical terms "Cure" and "Treatment". It has come to my attention that various people do not understand the difference behind the two terms which often led them into false hope. Therfore, I took it upon myself to create a website where I am able to guide my audience and help them understand the two terms with more depth.



Freedom Project

The freedom project is a project where we get to choose a topic we would like to further study and use our coding skills and character skills throughout it. We have spent time working on it in class and at home. Throughout the first semester we have used the skills we learned in class to create the freedom project. Some character skills we used are collaboration, agency. My freedom project revolves around the medical field.
